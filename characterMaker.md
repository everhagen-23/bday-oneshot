# GAME INFO
fight against superpowered vampires as a vampire slayer! Vampires are more powerful than mere mortals, so use a combination of cunning, trickery, and precision to deal the final blow, or just hit it til it falls down. 

# Making a Character
- roll 4d6 and drop the lowest for each stat (like D&D) (STRENCGTH,PERCEPTION, SUPPORT, AGILITY)
- pick a class
    - STRONGMAN:
        +2 strength
        choose 1 weapon from the STRONGMAN list
    - RANGER:
        +2 perception
        choose 1 weapon from the RANGER list
    - SUPPORTER: 
        +2 support
        choose 2 weapons/items from the SUPPORTER list
    - TRAPPER:
        +2 agility
        choose 2 weapons/items from the TRAPPER list

- Armor Class (AC) = perception modifer x 3 
- Health (HP) = support x 3
- Speed (SP)= agility x 3 
- Pick one item of clothing
