# GAME INFO
Welcome to Canin, where you are a vampire hunter in a world of superpowered vampires. You have been called to a small town for one reason or another to track down and kill a vampire, and the methods you use are up to you! 

# Making a Character

- roll 4d6 and drop the lowest for each stat (like D&D) (STRENGTH (STR),PERCEPTION (PC), SUPPORT (SP), AGILITY (AGIL))
- pick a class
    - STRONGMAN:
        +2 strength
        choose 1 weapon from the STRONGMAN list
    - RANGER:
        +2 perception
        choose 1 weapon from the RANGER list
    - SUPPORTER: 
        +2 support
        choose 2 weapons/items from the SUPPORTER list
    - TRAPPER:
        +2 agility
        choose 2 weapons/items from the TRAPPER list

- Armor Class (AC) = perception modifer x 3 
- Health (HP) = support x 3
- Speed (SP)= agility x 3 
- Pick one item of clothing (you can wear max of 2 at once)
- Start with 10 Coins and a VAMPIRE GUIDE (vampguide.pdf)
